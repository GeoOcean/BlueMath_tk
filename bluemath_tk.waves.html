bluemath_tk.waves.series.waves_dispersion(T, h)[source]

Solve the wave dispersion relation to calculate the wavelength (L), wave number (k), and phase speed (c). :param T: Wave period in seconds. :type T: float :param h: Water depth in meters. :type h: float

Returns:

  • L (float) – Wavelength in meters.

  • k (float) – Wave number in radians per meter.

  • c (float) – Phase speed in meters per second.
